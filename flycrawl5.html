Pancake

Who wants a pancake,
Sweet and piping hot?
Good little Grace looks up and says,
"I'll take the one on top."
Who else wants pancake,
Fresh off the griddle?
Terrible Theresa smiles and says,
"I'll take the one in the middle." Boxes

Then you must be my brother.

And we must not get thiner."
And so the two boxes hand in hand,

I've done it, I've done it! Guess what I've done!
Invented a light that plugs into the sun.
The sun is bright enough,
The bulb is strong enough,
But, oh, there is only one thing wrong...
